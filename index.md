Welcome! This repository hosts a series of original chemistry handouts to go along with Alchemy Learning's advanced chemistry lectures/videos. You should have a baseline knowledge of high-school chemistry (or AP), as this curriculum is designed to dive deeper into Olympiad and undergraduate chemistry. They're completely free and designed for personal/educational use only. You can access the video lectures to go along with these worksheets on YouTube.

I recommend following the units in order for a unified experience, but they can also be done standalone.
